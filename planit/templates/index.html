What is Data-Driven Instruction?

Tracking Standards and Objectives

Tell us what your weekly objective is and we will match it to the standards for your classroom even across mixed grade levels. See what standrds you've addressed, when, and how well your students performed.

Tracking Student Performance

Class Planit generates digital quizzes and worksheets based on your lesson plans. Easily assign these to your students as homework or manually enter assessment grades in your Class Planit gradebook.

Tracking Student Learning Styles

Using AI, Class Planit gives you valuable insights into the levels of Bloom's Taxonomy as well as the Learning Styles you address in your planning that are the most engaging to your students.

Streamline Data Collection

Class Planit allows you to gather insights in one place. Import/Export Gradebooks you create on our platform or connect to Google Sheets.

Allow Student and Teacher Reflection

With Student Self-Reflection and Teacher Notes, Class Planit combines Quantitative and Qualitative Data to gather a full picture of your classroom.

Implementing best teaching practices can be time consuming and overwelming. With Class Planit, we collect all the data to empower you to engage your students more effectively. Our Activity Recomendations and AI Generated Worksheets will save you time and and give you peace of mind knowing the lessons you choose are relavent to your educational standards and Weekly Objectives.

Get Started

Create

personalized lesson plans instantly
Simply answer a few questions and we'll generate a ready-to-use plan tailored to your needs

Creating a lesson plan can be simple, but making a lesson plan effective requires a lot of time and dedication. Class Planit takes into consideration a teacher's goal objectives, education standards, and targeted skills that their students need to reach each week.

Our custom lesson plans include the following resources:

  • Suggested video links based on the objective
  • Vocabulary terms built from the lesson topic
  • Editable tools to add activities, images, or additional learning materials

Measure

student progress and standards addressed
We'll track how your plans are meeting local education standards and learning styles

Our data-driven instruction tools make note of your objectives to recommend you personalized lessons to improve student engagement. We'll keep track of the goals your lessons meet, while you spend more time evaluating your students' performances.

Class Planit uses the following educational measurements:

  • Bloom's Taxonomy
  • The Seven Learning Styles
  • Adaptive Learning

Engage

students by connecting them to your lessons
Easily invite students to complete your lessons and keep track of their progress

Student engagement is the key to a successful lesson plan. Whether students are interacting with your lessons in class or online, Class Planit can provide the essential resources to meet your students' needs.

Class Planit helps you target your students' learning styles by providing:

  • Engaging lessons and activities
  • Teacher-student responses
  • Interactive badges
