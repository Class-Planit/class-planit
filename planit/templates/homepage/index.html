Align to Standards

Simply tell us what your weekly objective is and we will align it to your state/regional standards. We also track all the standards you have addressed from any planning platform including Google Drive.

Personalized Lessons

Based on your objectives, student performance, and Teaching Best Practices, we create activity recommendations that will be engaging and effective.

Digital Resources

Class Planit converts your lesson into a digital worksheet. Using your lessons, we pull out key terms and topics and create digital worksheets, study guides, and flashcards.

Key Features:

Create Awesome Lessons with Personalized Digital Resources in MINUTES not Hours!

Implement Teaching Best Practices:

As you plan your lesson, Class Planit analyzes how closely aligned it is to standard objectives, Bloom's levels, Learning Styles, as well as alignment to backwards design.

Add Personalized Activities:

Based on your objectives and the demographics of your students, Class Planit will make recommendations on activities you can include in your lesson.

Automatically Generated Worksheets

Based on your lessons, Class Planit will generate digital resources for your students. All you have to do is send out the assignment link and you are done!
