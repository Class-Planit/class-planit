What is Data-Driven Instruction?

Tracking Standards and Objectives

Tell us what your weekly objective is and we will match it to the standards for your classroom even across mixed grade levels. See what standrds you've addressed, when, and how well your students performed.

Tracking Student Performance

Class Planit generates digital quizzes and worksheets based on your lesson plans. Easily assign these to your students as homework or manually enter assessment grades in your Class Planit gradebook.

Tracking Student Learning Styles

Using AI, Class Planit gives you valuable insights into the levels of Bloom's Taxonomy as well as the Learning Styles you address in your planning that are the most engaging to your students.

Streamline Data Collection

Class Planit allows you to gather insights in one place. Import/Export Gradebooks you create on our platform or connect to Google Sheets.

Allow Student and Teacher Reflection

With Student Self-Reflection and Teacher Notes, Class Planit combines Quantitative and Qualitative Data to gather a full picture of your classroom.

Implementing best teaching practices can be time consuming and overwelming. With Class Planit, we collect all the data to empower you to engage your students more effectively. Our Activity Recomendations and AI Generated Worksheets will save you time and and give you peace of mind knowing the lessons you choose are relavent to your educational standards and Weekly Objectives.
